Bed, Breakfast, and Beautiful Scenery!

Small Skies B&B is a cozy two room Bed and Breakfast, on a property with both the benefits of city life and country nights. Spend your days enjoying tourist attractions in town, and come back after the sun sets to enjoy the stars in a clean and quiet environment, or take a retreat from everything from a few days to enjoy the fresh air and sunshine!

Whatever brings you to Small Skies B&B, we are here to accomodate, with fresh rooms and hot food for the duration of your stay.
