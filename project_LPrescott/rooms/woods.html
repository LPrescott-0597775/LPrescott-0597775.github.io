Room Info

This room is only a couple hundred feet away from Freedom Nature Trails, and in collaboration with them, comes with a pass for full access to their properties for the entirety of your stay! The trails can be accessed through an exclusive entry point in the yard.

The room is 1bed/1bath, with an entertainment console including a 45 inch TV, a kitchen area with a mini-fridge and microwave, walk-in closet, and amenities such as clean towels, shampoo, soap, toothpaste, and an in room coffee maker.
