Events

On the second and fourth Saturday of every month, Small Skies B&B hosts weekend events for guests to participate in, regardless of room and package. There are two events, one in the morning, from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM, and one in the evening, from 8:00 PM to 10:00 PM.

Morning EventsEvening Events
Second SaturdayBeginner and Intermediate level yoga classes Bonfire and hotdog cookout (vegan options available)
Fourth SaturdayFamily friendly Brunch and Crafts session Nature Documentary screening under the stars

For more information about upcoming events, please reach out! event plans (crafts, films, routines, and menus) are finalized a month in advance. Information about special events will also be included with reservation confirmations when applicable.
